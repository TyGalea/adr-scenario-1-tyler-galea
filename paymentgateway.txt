Context: Determining the best payment gateway for the mobile app.

Options Considered:
• Square
• Moneris
• PayPal

Decision:
The decision is to use PayPal for the mobile app.

Rationale
• Payments will be secure.
• Has a wide reach for international users as it accepts a variety of currencies.
• Simple and accessible for users.

Consequences
• Inconsistent usage fees.
• PayPal is lacking when it comes to customer service.
